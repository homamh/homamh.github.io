Hello,

I was born in the capital of Iraq, Baghdad. I didn't spend much of my life there and moved to a small town in Sweden where I started and finished primary school. My aspiration was to become a dentist, I played a lot of football and enjoyed every bit of life.

Life took an unexpected turn, in late 2007 I moved to Manchester, UK. It was my first time in high-school and I jumped straight into year 9 but eventually that part of life was done and I did Games Development for two years at The Manchester College. Later I did Digital SFX Animation at Edge Hill University and earlier this year I graduated with a BA degree.

Since mid-June 2016, I spent much of my time travelling and exploring inspiration to kick start my career. Being passionate and interested in motion design and branding I wish to excel in this line of work.

I like chillhop music, sci-fi and a whole lot of tv-series. Galacticos fanatic and skateboard newbie.
